"In jest, I once asked a close poet friend:
“So, will you write a poem
Even as you breathe your last?”
He looked at me and, with a smile, said:
“It’s impossible to write one before you die, my friend.
I die a million deaths for every poem I write.”
-
Gulzar translated by Nirupama Dutt"
